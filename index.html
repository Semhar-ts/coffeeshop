The Roasted Boon Co.

The Roasted Boon Co.

Start Your Sunrise at Washington

The Roasted Boon is becoming a new go-to for many in the area when they’re craving high-quality coffee. This unique coffee shop has a super unique vibe, with concrete floors and painted brick walls covered with stunning murals of the world map and images and decor from the owner’s home country of Eritrea in Africa. If you don’t want to sip your coffee indoors, they have an expansive patio outside for all customers to enjoy.
The interior of the Roasted Boon Co. roastery in Shaw, DC is decorated with a white Diedrich coffee roaster and colorful splashes and nods to the Eritrean “boon,” or coffee-making ceremony. The Boon (not bean) on its sign refers to a traditional coffee-making ceremony in Eritrea. This new coffee shop is brewing love and lattes in the Shaw neighborhood, and is already a popular spot for its spacious seating areas and cool ambiance.

OUR COFFEE

Each bean is carefully roasted right in the shop with their state-of-the-art coffee roaster which enhances the flavor of coffee beans from across the globe that then go right to your cup! If coffee isn’t your style, regulars rave about their icy frappes, smoothies, and the Eritrean Tea filled with rich spices And that’s before you even taste the drinks! The Roasted Boon is an Eritrean-owned coffee shop with drinks sourced from that region of East Africa as well as other fair-trade coops.
